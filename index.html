About INJOREP

The International Journal of Religions and Peacebuilding (INJOREP) is committed to publishing cutting-edge research in religious studies, peacebuilding, and the humanities & social sciences. As a peer-reviewed and interdisciplinary journal, we aim to foster dialogue, critical scholarship, and collaborative research on topics that address religious harmony, conflict resolution, and intercultural understanding.
