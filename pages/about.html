About INJOREP

Scope of the Journal

International Journal of Religions and Peacebuilding (INJOREP) is a peer-reviewed academic journal providing a platform for researchers to publish original research in religious peacebuilding, conflict resolution, humanitarian studies, and broader interdisciplinary fields in the humanities and social sciences. We encourage manuscripts that:

  • Explore religious perspectives on peace, conflict, and resolution.
  • Examine intercultural and interreligious dialogues.
  • Address political, sociological, and philosophical dimensions of faith and conflict.
  • Contribute fresh insights to the global discourse on peacebuilding.

Discipline

INJOREP primarily belongs to Humanities and Social Sciences research areas (including but not limited to Religious Studies, Peace Studies, Political Science, Sociology, Anthropology, and related fields).
