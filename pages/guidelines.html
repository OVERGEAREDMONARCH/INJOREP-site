Author Guidelines

Preparing Your Manuscript

When submitting a manuscript to INJOREP, please ensure it follows these guidelines:

  • Language: Clear, concise English.
  • Format: 12 pt font, double-spaced; include Title, Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, and References.
  • Reference Style: APA or Harvard (be consistent throughout the manuscript).
  • Figures/Tables: Number all figures/tables consecutively and cite them in the text.

Submission Checklist

  • All authors have approved the final version.
  • Work is original and not published elsewhere.
  • Manuscript meets ethical guidelines (especially for research involving human or animal subjects).
