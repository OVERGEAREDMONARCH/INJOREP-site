Review Policy

Peer-Review Process

INJOREP follows a double-blind peer-review process. Submissions are evaluated based on originality, clarity, significance, and methodological rigor.

  • Initial screening by Editor-in-Chief or assigned Editor.
  • At least two independent reviewers with subject expertise.
  • Evaluation of ethical standards, plagiarism check, and data integrity.

Timeline

We aim to complete the review within 4–6 weeks. Authors are expected to revise and resubmit promptly if requested. The Editor-in-Chief makes the final publication decision.

Ethical Considerations

INJOREP adheres to strict ethical guidelines. Any detected misconduct (plagiarism, data falsification, etc.) may result in immediate rejection or retraction of the manuscript.
