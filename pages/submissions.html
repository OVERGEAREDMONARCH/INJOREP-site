Submissions

Call for Papers

INJOREP welcomes high-quality manuscripts focusing on religious peacebuilding, interfaith dialogue, conflict transformation, and other humanities/social science perspectives related to peace studies. We accept:

  • Original Research Articles
  • Review Papers
  • Short Communications
  • Case Studies

Submission Guidelines

Before submitting your manuscript, please ensure it aligns with the journal’s scope and follows the Author Guidelines. Submissions undergo a double-blind peer review. For additional details, visit our Editorial Board page.
